Welcome to TicTacToe-akshigames

Playing Instructions:

  • Take turns placing X and O symbols in empty squares.
  • Aim to get three symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • The first player to achieve three in a row wins, or it's a draw if the grid fills up without a winner.
